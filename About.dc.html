About Us

An independent bridge between two thriving regions

The ASEAN Australia Association is a member-led organisation connecting the businesses, institutions and people building the future of the region, together.

Mission

To help members build trusted relationships and create real opportunities across ASEAN and Australia.

Vision

A region where business, ideas and people move freely, and prosperity is shared.

Who we are

Independent, non-partisan and member-led

We are not a government body or a consulting firm. We are a community of businesses, professionals, entrepreneurs, investors, universities and institutions who believe the ASEAN–Australia relationship is one of the most important of the century.

Our work is simple: open doors, share knowledge and connect businesses, leaders and communities across ASEAN and Australia.
